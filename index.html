FAQs

Do Carteon Cards work with all smartphones?

+

Yes, our cards work with all NFC-enabled smartphones (Android and iPhone with NFC support) and include a QR code for universal compatibility.

Can I update my details after I get my card?

+

Absolutely! Your digital profile can be updated anytime through your Carteon dashboard without needing a new physical card.

What makes Carteon better than paper business cards?

+

Carteon cards are eco-friendly, allow instant sharing, can be updated anytime, and provide rich analytics on how often your card is shared.

How do I share my details with someone who doesn't have NFC?

+

Each Carteon card includes a QR code that anyone can scan with their smartphone camera to access your digital profile.

Do I need an app to use Carteon Cards?

+

No app required! NFC works directly with your phone's built-in functionality, and QR codes can be scanned with any camera.

Can I include more than just my phone number?

+

Yes, you can include your website, social media profiles, email, address, calendar link, and any other contact information you choose.

What if I change jobs or phone numbers?

+

Simply log into your Carteon dashboard and update your information. Your physical card stays the same, but your digital profile updates instantly.

How durable are the cards?

+

Our cards are made from premium materials including metal, PVC, and wood. They're designed to withstand daily use and last for years.

Can I order for my entire team or company?

+

Yes, we offer bulk ordering for teams and companies with custom branding options and volume discounts.
